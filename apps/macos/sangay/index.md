## ✨ Core Features

### Live Menu Bar Status
Keep track of your storage right where you need it. Customize the display mode to show Free %, Free GB, Used GB, or just a simple status dot.

### Smart Alerts
Receive warnings at customizable thresholds (Warning, Critical, Emergency). Control notifications and sounds per alert level, or pause them temporarily when you're busy.

### Dashboard & Disk History
View a larger summary of your disk state, including 24-hour, 7-day, and 30-day storage history charts.

### Preview-First Cleanup
When it's time to free space, Sangay builds a cleanup preview first. It separates low-risk actions from review-carefully targets, and *only* runs cleanup after you confirm. Built-in presets cover Caches, Logs, Temporary Files, Trash, and Developer Caches.

### Large-Item Discovery
Scan your home folder for the largest files and folders. Reveal them in Finder and decide what to keep and what to discard.

## 🔒 Privacy & Safety Model

*   **Local-First:** Sangay is designed to keep operational data on-device.
*   **Explicit Confirmation:** Manual cleanup is always preview-first. Nothing is deleted until you confirm.
*   **Risk Labeling:** Automatic scheduled cleanup is limited to low-risk targets. Trash and developer caches are treated as review-carefully targets.
*   **Accurate Reporting:** Deletion failures are reported instead of being silently ignored.

## 💻 Requirements

*   macOS 14 or newer
*   Full Disk Access (requested only when entering cleanup workflows)
